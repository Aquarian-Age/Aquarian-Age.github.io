nmcli

connection选项

　
链接到已知历史网络实用

显示所有网络

nmcli connection show


链接到历史网络

up [[id | uuid | path] ] [ifname ] [ap ] [passwd-file ]

  • 示例
    nmcli connection up 1101

  • 取消链接
    nmcli connection down 1101

  • 使用密码链接
    nmcli connection –ask up HTC\ Portable\ Hotspot\ 830B

  • nmcli connection show

device选项

添加新的网络链接　扫描网络实用

  • 查看wifi是否启用
    ~
    host@user 25 12:10 >nmcli radio wifi
    enabled
    ~
    host@user 26 12:11 >nmcli radio wifi on

  • 关闭wifi
    ~
    host@user 27 12:11 >nmcli radio wifi off
    ~
    host@user 28 12:11 >nmcli radio wifi
    disabled

  • 启用wifi
    ~
    host@user 29 12:11 >nmcli radio wifi on
    ~
    host@user 30 12:11 >nmcli radio wifi
    enabled

  • 列出当前可用wifi
    ~
    host@user 31 12:11 >nmcli device wifi list
